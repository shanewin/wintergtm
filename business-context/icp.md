# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

## Firmographics

- **Industry / vertical:** Field Service Management (FSM) software companies that sell to HVAC contractors (and adjacent trades: plumbing, electrical, garage door, etc.)
- **Company size:** Small to mid-market — roughly 10–200 employees. NOT the dominant players (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber) who have internal data/GTM engineering teams.
- **Revenue range:** ~$2M–$50M ARR (estimated)
- **Geography:** US-based or US-market-focused (our signal pipeline covers any US metro)
- **Business model:** B2B SaaS, selling subscriptions to home service contractors

## Buyer personas

### Primary: Founder / CEO
- **When they're the buyer:** Companies under ~30 employees where the founder still owns sales strategy and pipeline
- **Cares about:** Revenue growth, competitive edge, proving the product wins in specific metros, efficient use of limited sales headcount

### Primary: VP Sales / Head of Revenue / CRO
- **When they're the buyer:** Companies with 30–200 employees that have a dedicated sales org
- **Cares about:** Lead quality, conversion rate, pipeline coverage in target metros, arming BDRs with better intel than a cold list

### Blocker: Head of Product / Engineering
- **Why they block:** May view external signal work as something they should build internally
- **Mitigation:** Position as faster-to-value than an internal build; we deliver week 1, their team can't for months

## Qualifying signals

- **Actively selling to HVAC contractors** (or adjacent trades) as a core segment
- **Has outbound sales motion** — BDRs/SDRs or at minimum a founder doing outbound
- **No internal data engineering team** building custom lead scoring or signal pipelines
- **Expanding into new metros** or trying to grow market share in existing ones
- **Hiring signals:** Posting for BDR/SDR/AE roles (means they're investing in outbound)
- **Recently funded:** Seed through Series B — have budget, under pressure to grow

## Disqualifiers

- Has an internal data team or GTM engineering function
- Enterprise-only (sells to national chains, not local contractors)
- No outbound motion and no interest in starting one
- Dominant market player with 500+ employees

## Current awareness

- **How they describe the problem:** "Our BDRs waste time calling contractors who aren't buying" / "We don't know which contractors in a metro are actually in-market" / "Apollo gives us a list but no signal"
- **What they've tried:** Apollo/ZoomInfo for firmographic lists, buying lists from trade associations, referral programs, trade show lead capture
- **Where they go for advice:** SaaStr, RevGenius, sales Twitter/LinkedIn, FSM industry events (Digital Wrap Conference, etc.)
- **Gap we fill:** They can get a list of HVAC contractors in Phoenix from Apollo. They CANNOT get a scored, prioritized list showing which ones are in pain right now based on review complaints, dispatch hiring, review velocity, missing FSM platforms, and license records. That's the deliverable.
